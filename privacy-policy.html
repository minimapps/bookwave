BookWave Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026

BookWave is an iOS app for reading EPUB books, listening with text-to-speech, and tracking reading progress. This Privacy Policy explains how BookWave handles information.

Information BookWave Stores

BookWave may store information needed for app functionality, including your imported books, reading progress, highlights, bookmarks, listening activity, app settings, and library metadata.

This information is stored locally on your device and may be stored or synced using Apple services such as iCloud, CloudKit, or iCloud Drive, depending on your device settings and how you use the app.

Imported Books and User Content

BookWave is designed to let you import and read EPUB files. Your imported books and reading data are used only to provide app features such as reading, text-to-speech, progress tracking, highlights, bookmarks, and library storage.

BookWave does not sell your imported books, reading activity, highlights, bookmarks, or personal reading data.

Subscriptions and Purchases

BookWave may offer auto-renewable subscriptions through Apple In-App Purchase. Payments and subscription management are handled by Apple.

BookWave may use RevenueCat to manage subscription status and purchase entitlements. RevenueCat may process purchase-related information, such as app user identifiers, product identifiers, transaction information, and entitlement status, for the purpose of enabling premium features.

Analytics and Tracking

BookWave does not use third-party advertising tracking and does not sell personal data.

If analytics or diagnostic information is collected through Apple or other services, it is used to understand app performance, fix issues, and improve the app.

Apple Services

BookWave may rely on Apple services such as iCloud, CloudKit, iCloud Drive, StoreKit, and system text-to-speech. Your use of Apple services is also subject to Apple's privacy policies and your Apple account settings.

Children's Privacy

BookWave is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

Data Deletion

You can delete imported books, highlights, bookmarks, and app data from within the app where supported. You may also remove app data from your device or iCloud using iOS and iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

BookWave

Read and listen to your EPUB books on iPhone and iPad.

BookWave turns your EPUB collection into a beautiful reading and listening experience — no accounts, no clutter, just your books.

Features

  • Import your own books. Add any DRM-free EPUB and it appears on your shelf in seconds.
  • Listen to any book. Natural on-device voices read your books aloud, with each word highlighted as it is spoken. Playback continues in the background.
  • Create local audio files. Turn imported EPUBs into shareable audio files using on-device voices.
  • Everything in sync. Your library, reading progress, highlights, and bookmarks sync privately across your devices through iCloud — no sign-up required.
  • Highlight and remember. Highlights and bookmarks stay with the book on all your devices.
  • Track your reading. See how much you read and listen, day by day.
  • 20 classics included. Start right away with a free built-in library of timeless books.
  • Private by design. No accounts, no tracking. Your books and reading data live on your device and in your personal iCloud — nowhere else.

BookWave is free to download. Reading and importing books is free, and you can listen to all built-in classics at no cost. BookWave Pro, an optional subscription, unlocks listening for your imported books, local audio export for imported books, and detailed reading statistics.

Support

How do I import a book?

Tap the add button on the shelf and pick any DRM-free .epub file from the Files app. The book appears on your shelf right away.

How does listening work?

Open a book and start playback. BookWave reads the text aloud using voices that run entirely on your device — nothing is sent to any server. You can keep listening with the screen off or while using other apps.

How does audio export work?

Long-press a local EPUB in your library and choose Generate Audio File. BookWave creates the audio locally on your device and opens the share sheet so you can save or send the file.

How does sync work?

When you are signed in to iCloud, your library, reading progress, highlights, and bookmarks sync automatically across your iPhone and iPad through your private iCloud account. There is no BookWave account and no third-party server.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open Settings inside BookWave and tap Restore Purchases. The same option is also available on the BookWave Pro screen.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open the iOS Settings app → your name → Subscriptions, or see Apple's guide to managing subscriptions. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual books, highlights, and bookmarks inside the app. BookWave also offers an option in Settings to delete your synced library data from iCloud across all your devices.

Where do the built-in classics come from?

BookWave includes a small starter library of classic books that are believed to be public domain or otherwise lawfully available for inclusion in the app. Public domain status may vary by country or region. If you believe a work has been included incorrectly, please contact us.

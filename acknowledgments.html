Starter Library

BookWave includes a small starter library of classic EPUB books so new users can try reading and listening immediately. These works are believed to be public domain or otherwise lawfully available for inclusion in the app.

Public domain and copyright status can vary by country or region. If you believe a work has been included incorrectly, please contact us at minimapps.support@icloud.com.

Note: User-imported books are not provided or authorized by BookWave. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to import, store, and use their own EPUB files.
